## Overview

Before investing significant resources, you need confidence that customers actually want what you're building. This framework provides a systematic approach to validation.

## Core Components

### 1. Problem Validation

- Identify and interview target users
- Validate that the problem is real and urgent
- Quantify the market opportunity

### 2. Solution Validation

- Test your proposed solution approach
- Run small experiments and prototypes
- Gather feedback from early users

### 3. Business Model Validation

- Confirm customers will pay
- Understand pricing sensitivity
- Map revenue mechanics

### 4. Market Timing

- Assess market readiness
- Understand competitive landscape
- Identify entry strategies
