## Overview

Design thinking is a structured approach to innovation that emphasizes empathy, ideation, and rapid iteration. This workshop introduces the core methodology used in both corporate innovation and startup contexts.

## What You'll Learn

- Understanding the five stages of design thinking
- Empathy mapping and user research techniques
- Ideation and prototyping methods
- Testing and iteration cycles

## Activities

- Live case study analysis
- Small group design challenge
- Prototype creation exercise
- Feedback and iteration practice
