## Overview

This course covers the principles, practices, and tools that enable teams to build software systems that are reliable, maintainable, and scalable.

## Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course, participants will:

- Understand core software engineering principles
- Apply design patterns effectively
- Write testable, maintainable code
- Design systems for scale
- Lead technical decision-making

## Course Structure

Hands-on labs, design exercises, and real-world case studies form the backbone of this course. Students work individually and in teams to solve increasingly complex engineering challenges.

## Who Should Attend

- Software developers and engineers
- Technical leads and architects
- Teams building new systems or modernizing existing ones
- Anyone seeking to deepen their engineering fundamentals
