## Overview

Corporate Innovation is designed for leaders, managers, and innovators who want to drive meaningful change within established organizations.

## Key Focus Areas

- Strategic frameworks for innovation
- Building and sustaining innovation cultures
- Managing innovation portfolios
- Measuring innovation success

## Course Structure

Participants will engage with real-world case studies, develop innovation strategies, and create actionable innovation roadmaps.

## Who Should Attend

- Corporate executives and managers
- Innovation leaders and change agents
- Teams tasked with driving organizational transformation
- Professionals seeking to understand innovation at scale
