## Overview

NUvention Web is an intensive, hands-on course where student teams conceptualize, build, and launch real web and mobile ventures within a single quarter (20 weeks).

## Program Goals

- Launch a working product to real users
- Develop practical entrepreneurial skills
- Understand product-market fit and iteration
- Build a functional startup team

## What Makes NUvention Unique

- Real ventures with real capital and market validation
- Small team sizes for meaningful ownership
- Expert mentorship from practitioners
- Tight feedback loops and rapid iteration

## Who Should Enroll

- Aspiring entrepreneurs
- Technical founders and product builders
- Students interested in startup mechanics
- Teams with a clear product idea ready to execute
