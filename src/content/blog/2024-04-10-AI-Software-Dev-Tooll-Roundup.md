## Summary

This post is a survey of a few popular AI software development assistance, and where to go to get started with them in VS Code. It doesn't provide a review or opinion. Personally, i have been using both Phind and Github Copilot (as well as just straight chatgpt) and together they are a big productivity boost from anything to designing initial data structures, deriving types in typescript, and generating unit tests in vitest or jest.
